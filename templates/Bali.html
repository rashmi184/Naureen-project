Travel to Bali

Reason to Travel

One of the most popular destinations in the world, Bali is blessed with an abundance of must-visit attractions. Bali is an Indonesian island known for its amazing pristine bone-white sand beaches as well as for its stunning black sand volcanic beaches. It is appreciated for its rich culture and heritage .Bali is a super budget-friendly destination and surprisingly still affordable if we know where to look. The island is home to religious sites, yoga and meditation retreats. Bali should be visited by each and everyone for its natural beauty. Bali has many surprises for a traveller to be stunned . Also travellers, please don't forget to do shopping in Bali .

Places to visit

  1. Pura Luhur Lempuyang
  2. Diamond Beach
  3. Campuhan Ridge Walk
  4. Sukawati Art Market
  5. Sekumpul Waterfalls
  6. Challenge Yourself by the Swings
  7. Tegallalang and Jatiluwih Rice Terraces in Bali
  8. Pura Ulun Danu Bratan

Cons of Bali

1. Traffic
2. Transportation
3. Getting Visa is time consuming and expensive. Indian tourists do not need any visa. They just need to get a Visa Exemption Stamp from the immigration counter.

If you are convinced to travel to this Surprising Place, then book your tickets and enjoy your holidays!!!
