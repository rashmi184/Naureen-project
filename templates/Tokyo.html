Travel to Tokyo

Reason to Travel

Tokyo, the world's most populous metropolitan area., is a combination of both modern and traditional sites. Tokyo is a friendly and welcoming country, steep in history and tradition. The culture of this country is an interesting blend of Eastern traditions and Western modernity that can be seen everywhere. There are countless independent fashion boutiques in the Harajuku area all dedicated to youth fashion. It has the world's largest fashion district: featuring basically every single major designer brand in the world. The electronics stores are like theme parks. We can ride the world's shortest escalator here and a world full of comics .

  • Mount Fuji

  • Kyoto Rail Tour by Bullet Train
