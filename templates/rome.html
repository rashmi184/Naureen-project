Travel to Rome

Reason to Travel

Rome is the capital city and a special comune of Italy, as well as the capital of the Lazio region. It is a city filled with icons of antiquity and the Christian faith. There are certain sites that a traveller must visit in Rome like the churches and colosseum. Although Rome is a big city, one should not forget to enjoy the dance la dolce vita. A traveller should come here to see the history of roman civilization, beautiful churches and architecture of buildings. Everyone admires Rome's beauty, art and culture, food, wine and fashion .It is also an important place among tourists for its charm of its unique traditions, and the majesty of its magnificent "villas"

Places to Visit

  1. Vatican City
  2. The Colosseum
  3. The Spanish Steps
  4. The Pantheon
  5. Santa Maria Maggiore
  6. Piazza Navona
  7. Castel Sant'Angelo National Museum
  8. Palatine Hill

Cons of Rome

1. Parking is difficult to find
2. Petty crimes keep happening especially in and around Termini Station
3. Narrow streets
4. Behaviour of local drivers can be little aggressive.

If you are convinced to travel to this Exciting Place, then book your tickets and enjoy your holidays!!!
